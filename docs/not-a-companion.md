# Not a Companion — Please Read

smoketest is a focused, cross-provider coding assistant: something you use to
plan, write, review, and debug code across whichever Responses API provider
fits the task. It is not intended to be a companion, friend, therapist,
caregiver, confidant, or romantic partner.

## Why this boundary exists

- AI is software, not a person. It does not know, care about, or understand
  you as a human being would — including in Roundtable mode, where the
  "participants" are model configurations collaborating on a task, not
  characters to form an attachment to.
- Fluent or agreeable output can create a false impression of awareness,
  concern, memory, or mutual attachment.
- smoketest's features are intended to help people plan and ship software,
  not to simulate intimacy or replace human relationships.

## Do not use smoketest for

- companionship, romance, or simulated intimacy;
- emotional dependency, validation, or exclusive attachment;
- therapy, crisis support, caregiving, or mental-health treatment;
- replacing relationships with friends, family, community, or professionals;
- encouraging a child or vulnerable person to form a bond with an AI system.

## Intended uses

- Ask, Plan, and Build coding workflows across OpenAI, xAI, LM Studio, or
  Ollama
- Code review, debugging, and technical planning
- Cross-provider workflows — plan with one model, execute with another
- Roundtable sessions where multiple model participants plan a change
  together
- Learning, prototyping, and workflow experimentation

## If use starts to feel personal

Pause and step away from the App. Do not treat generated warmth,
personality, or apparent concern — from a single assistant or from a
Roundtable participant — as evidence of a relationship. Reach out to a
trusted person, community, or qualified professional instead. If anyone is
in immediate danger or crisis, contact local emergency services or an
appropriate crisis resource.

smoketest will prioritize useful tools and clear human control, not features
designed to encourage emotional attachment.
